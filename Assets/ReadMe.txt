When you hit an enemy ball, it now goes away from the player.
When all enemies are destroyed a new wave will spawn.
After receiving powerup, will only last 5 seconds.
Enemies increment in number with each corresponding wave.
Ball moves towards players' goal.
Player can press spacebar for a turbo boost. Smoke trail will appear.
Enemies speed increases with each new wave.
